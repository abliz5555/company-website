Katanukiya - UAE

Katanukiya is a unique Japanese confectionery brand offering Baumkuchen cakes that can be enjoyed by “cutting out" cute characters printed on each cake. Designed with fun and gifting in mind, Katanukiya combines traditional Japanese quality with modern playful aesthetics.

Craftsmanship & Production:
• Carefully layered flat-style Baumkuchen, baked layer by layer to create beautiful textures
• Precision-cut using a specialized water-jet cutter, enabling intricate lines and detailed character shapes
• Each cake is printed along the cut lines for a playful and visually appealing design

Product Features:
• Flat-style Baumkuchen with printed character designs
• Edible cut-out lines allow consumers to “cut out" the character before eating
• Engaging and Instagrammable experience for children and adults alike

Packaging & Design:
• Beautifully boxed and individually wrapped
• Gift-friendly Japanese-style design with seasonal and character-based variants
• Licensed character collaborations in preparation (e.g., Sanrio)
• OEM and private-label options available for large orders

Track Record & Target Market:
• Approximately 4 million units sold annually in Japan
• Strong domestic sales at Tokyo Station, Haneda Airport, and major department stores
• Proven demand from international travelers and retailers
• Manufactured in-house to ensure quality control and timely supply
• High-end gift shops, airport duty-free stores, department stores, and tourist locations in Japan

New Signature Product - GildArt Golden Chocolate

Our latest creation, GildArt Golden Chocolate, is a luxurious confectionery crafted in the UAE. Each piece is coated with real 24K edible gold, applied using a unique Japanese technique that creates a flawless, smooth golden surface. On top of this gold layer, we use high-precision Japanese printing technology with food-safe edible ink to design and print detailed artwork directly onto the chocolate.

The result is more than just chocolate — it is a true piece of art. From the brilliant golden finish to the refined packaging, every detail of GildArt is crafted to deliver an experience of elegance and exclusivity. This product brings a completely new level of luxury to the chocolate market in the UAE.

We are also expanding the GildArt collection with new golden chocolate flavors coming soon, offering even more options for those who appreciate fine, artistic confectionery.
