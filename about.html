Our UAE Branch

Grape Tree FZE, established in 2024 in Sharjah Airport Free Zone, specializes in luxury confectionery products such as Katanukiya and GildArt Golden Chocolate. We bring Japanese craftsmanship to the UAE gifting market.

Our Parent Company

Budonomori Co. Ltd, established in 1982 in Ishikawa, Japan, operates across multiple domains including restaurants, weddings, confectionery, and specialty retail. Their hospitality and craftsmanship inspire all Grape Tree products.

Signature Product - Katanukiya

Katanukiya is our premium signature confectionery, crafted with attention to every detail. Designed for luxury gifting, it embodies the perfect balance of tradition, flavor, and artistry.

New Signature Product - GildArt Golden Chocolate

Our latest creation, GildArt Golden Chocolate, is a luxurious confection made for the sophisticated gift market. Each piece reflects elegance, indulgence, and a unique gifting experience.

Our Mission & Vision

We aim to provide unforgettable luxury gifting experiences by combining Japanese craftsmanship, premium ingredients, and modern design. Our vision is to be a leading brand in the high-end confectionery and gifting market worldwide.
