About Grape Tree FZE

Grape Tree FZE, established in 2024 in Sharjah Airport Free Zone, UAE, is a luxury gifting brand specializing in premium confectionery. Our signature products include Katanukiya and the new GildArt Golden Chocolate, crafted for the discerning luxury market.

We are proudly part of our parent company, Budonomori Co. Ltd, established in 1982 in Ishikawa, Japan. Rooted in the spirit of hospitality and craftsmanship, Budonomori operates across multiple business domains including restaurants, weddings, confectionery, and specialty retail.

With our unique products and attention to detail, Grape Tree FZE delivers unforgettable experiences through luxury gifting, combining the heritage of our Japanese parent company with modern innovation and creativity in the UAE.
