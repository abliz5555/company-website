About Us

Grape Tree FZE, a branch of Budounoki Co., Ltd. based in Japan, is renowned for its custom-made cakes and desserts. We specialize in crafting wholesale custom layer cakes, including intricately designed cut-out cakes and the famous golden castella cakes. With our expert craftsmanship and dedication to quality, we cater to both individual and corporate events, ensuring that every sweet creation is a true masterpiece.
