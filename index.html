About Us

Grape Tree FZE is the UAE branch of Budounoki Ltd., a leading Japanese company renowned for its premium-quality cakes and desserts. With roots in Japan, Budounoki Ltd. has a legacy of excellence, blending traditional techniques with modern innovation.

Our branch in the Sharjah Airport Free Zone specializes in producing and exporting unique custom cakes, ensuring the same level of craftsmanship and quality that Budounoki Ltd. is known for globally.
